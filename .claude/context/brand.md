# Brand & Positioning

## Business Name
Precision BMW Repair

## Positioning
Premier full-service repair and maintenance facility exclusively dedicated to BMW vehicles. Trusted experts — BMW drivers deserve high end service.

## Three Words
High End. Meticulous. Master Technician.

## Tone of Voice
Technical and authoritative. Confident, not arrogant. Direct and precise. Never salesy or pushy. Let credentials speak. Copy should feel like it was written by an engineer, not a marketer.

## Key Differentiators
- BMW-exclusive — we only work on BMWs, nothing else
- Dealer-trained technician — direct BMW of North America training since 1992
- Genuine OEM parts only — absolutely no aftermarket parts ever
- Dealer-level tools, equipment, and BMW-specific computers
- Warranty on all parts and labor
- You always work directly with the owner
